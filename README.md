# 🚀 Artificial Intelligence Projects - Ravion Lab

Welcome to the **AI Research & Development Hub** of Ravion Lab!  
Here, we focus on **machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and computer vision** to create innovative AI solutions.

## 📌 Vision & Mission
We aim to **revolutionize AI applications** by building **cutting-edge models** that solve real-world problems in fields like:
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
- **Computer Vision**
- **Speech Recognition & Translation**
- **AI-Powered Decision Systems**

## 🔥 Featured Projects
1. 🧠 **AI-Powered News Aggregator** – A system that collects and summarizes AI/ML research news.  
2. 🔊 **Real-time Speech Translator** – Translates any movie or YouTube video into multiple languages.  
3. 🤖 **AI Chatbot for Research Summarization** – Converts research papers into easy-to-read summaries.  

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenAI, Hugging Face**
- **Django/FastAPI for Backend**
- **MongoDB/PostgreSQL for Data Storage**
- **Docker & Kubernetes for Deployment**
